About GrowHome Calc

Home Design Breakeven Feedback Calculations

GrowHome Calc is a free and educational tool designed to help individuals and households explore the costs, yield potential, and economic feasibility of small-scale hydroponic systems.

Built using Python and Flask, GrowHome Calc empowers users to:

  • Design a personalized indoor farming setup
  • Estimate monthly yield and breakeven timelines
  • Download cost breakdowns and reports

Whether you're a sustainability enthusiast, student, or urban gardener, this app is intended to spark curiosity and make growing your own food more transparent and achievable.
