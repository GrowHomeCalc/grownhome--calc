GrowHome Calc is a tool for aspiring home growers, urban gardeners, and sustainability advocates. It helps you:

  • Estimate cost and yield of indoor leafy green hydroponic systems
  • Compare the cost-effectiveness of growing food at home vs. store-bought
  • Design your ideal home setup with rack, tray, and equipment options
  • Track breakeven timelines and sustainability metrics
