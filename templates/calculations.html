GrowHome Calc: Calculation Methods

This page explains the formulas and logic used throughout the GrowHome Calc tool.

1. Yield Calculation

Total Yield (lbs/month):

Yield = (Base Yield per Tray) × Number of Trays × Seasonal Multiplier × Sunlight Direction Multiplier

2. Electricity Cost

Monthly Electricity Cost = (Wattage × Hours/Day × Days/Month ÷ 1000) × kWh Rate

3. Nutrient Cost

Nutrient Cost = $10.00/month (Flat estimate based on average leafy green hydroponic needs)

4. Cost Per Pound

Cost Per Pound = (Electricity + Nutrients) ÷ Monthly Yield

5. Breakeven Estimate

Breakeven (Months) = System Build Cost ÷ Monthly Savings Compared to Average Store Price
