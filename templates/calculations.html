How We Calculate

  • Yield per Tray: Each tray = 2.8 sqft growing area × crop-specific lbs/sqft/month
  • Monthly Electricity Cost: Based on LED wattage × 16 hrs/day × seasonal & sunlight multipliers + 7.5 kWh air pump
  • Cost per Pound: (Electricity + Nutrients) ÷ Estimated Yield (lbs)
  • Breakeven Timeline: System Cost ÷ (Monthly Savings vs. store prices)
  • Build Cost: Sum of lights, pumps, structure, nutrients, reservoir, and optional sensors

Sunlight direction and seasonality affect LED usage. For example, a south-facing window in summer lowers energy needs.

Store prices are averages from local Maryland grocers as of April 2025.
