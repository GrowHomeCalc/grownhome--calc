Formulas Used

  • Monthly Yield (lbs):
    Monthly Yield = trays × 2.8 sqft × crop_yield_rate (lbs/sqft/month)
  • Electricity Use (kWh/month):
    LED kWh = (base_wattage × trays × season_multiplier × sunlight_multiplier) ÷ 1000 × 16 hrs/day × 30 days
    Total kWh = LED kWh + 7.5 kWh (air pump)
  • Electricity Cost ($):
    Electricity Cost = Total kWh × $0.161 (MD utility rate)
  • Cost Per Pound:
    Cost/lb = (Electricity + Nutrients) ÷ Monthly Yield
  • Monthly Savings:
    Monthly Savings = (Monthly Yield × Market Price/lb) − (Electricity + Nutrients)
  • Breakeven Timeline (months):
    Breakeven = System Cost ÷ Monthly Savings
    Only shown if Monthly Savings > 0
  • Build Cost:
    Total = Lighting + Pump + Structural + Nutrients + Sensors (optional)

Calculations are based on real-world hydroponic benchmarks and Maryland electricity prices as of April 2025.
