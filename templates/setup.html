Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Assemble rack and trays – Ensure each tray has water-tight connections and is supported evenly.
  2. Install reservoir – Place under the lowest tray; ensure 20–50 gal capacity based on your system.
  3. Connect air pump and tubing – Run airline tubes from the pump to each tray; include air stones.
  4. Add nutrients and pH test – Mix water with starter nutrients and check pH (ideal range: 5.5–6.5).
  5. Insert net pots and LECA – Fill with lightweight clay pebbles or chosen substrate.
  6. Install grow lights – Position LEDs ~6–12 inches above the canopy; set timers for 12–16 hours/day.
  7. Test flood & drain cycle – If applicable, ensure cycles run 15–30 min intervals, 3–4x/day.
  8. Monitor and adjust – Track EC, pH, water level weekly. Adjust lighting and airflow as plants grow.
